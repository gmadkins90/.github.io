Personal Background

I have a fairly diverse background, where I have worked in customer service, management, consumer IT, as well as military service. Throughout my career I noticed that one of my skills and enjoyment in life has been the ability to problem-solve, and a general dislike of static job duties; These are a large reason behind my love for software development. The diversity of individual software projects, and the inevitable issues that arise within coding, drew me to naturally enjoy programming!
